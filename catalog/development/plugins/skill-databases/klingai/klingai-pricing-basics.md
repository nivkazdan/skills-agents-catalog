### What This Skill Does

This skill explains Kling AI pricing and cost optimization strategies. It covers:

- Credit-based pricing model
- Cost factors (model, duration, resolution)
- Credit consumption calculation
- Budget planning and estimation
- Cost optimization strategies
- Free tier and quota understanding

### Technical Success Criteria

- Clear cost estimates and budget tracking
- Understanding of credit consumption factors
- Cost calculation implemented in code

### Business Success Criteria

- Controlled spending within budget limits
- Predictable costs for planning
- Optimized credit utilization
