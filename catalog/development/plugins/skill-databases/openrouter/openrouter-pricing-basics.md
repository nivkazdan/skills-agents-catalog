## Summary

**Category:** onboarding
**Target Audience:** Developer or project manager
**Trigger Phrases:** `openrouter pricing`, `openrouter cost`, `how much openrouter`, `openrouter credits`

### What This Skill Does

This skill explains OpenRouter pricing and cost optimization strategies. It covers:

- Per-token pricing model
- Price comparison across 100+ models
- Input vs output token costs
- Credit system and prepaid credits
- Cost optimization strategies
- Budget planning and estimation

### Technical Success Criteria

- Clear cost estimates for different models
- Understanding of token-based pricing
- Cost calculation implemented in code

### Business Success Criteria

- Controlled spending within budget limits
- Predictable costs for planning
- Optimal cost-quality trade-offs
