## Summary

**Category:** Enterprise
**Target Audience:** Engineering managers, Platform administrators, Team leads
**Trigger Phrases:** ideogram team access, ideogram permissions, ideogram usage policy, ideogram team setup

This skill configures team-based access and usage policies for shared Ideogram resources. It covers API key management per team, usage quota allocation, cost tracking per team, audit logging, and policy enforcement. Use this skill when multiple teams share Ideogram resources and need fair usage allocation and cost accountability.
