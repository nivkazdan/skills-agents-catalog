## Summary

**Category:** Onboarding
**Target Audience:** All developers, AI engineers, Product managers
**Trigger Phrases:** ideogram mistakes, ideogram pitfalls, ideogram common issues, ideogram anti-patterns

This skill documents common mistakes and anti-patterns when working with the Ideogram API. It covers pitfalls like improper prompt structure, ignoring aspect ratio constraints, not implementing retry logic, missing cost tracking, and typography rendering issues. Use this skill proactively when designing Ideogram integrations to avoid common problems.
