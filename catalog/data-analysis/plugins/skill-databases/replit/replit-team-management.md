## File Descriptions

### 1. SKILL.md
**Purpose:** Configure Replit Teams with member roles, permissions, shared secrets, and collaborative workflows.
**Workflow:** Team administration skill - establishes organizational structure for collaborative development.
**Relates to:** Enables replit-multiplayer-collab; integrates with replit-secrets-management for shared credentials.

## Summary

This skill configures Replit Teams for organizational development. It covers creating and managing Team workspaces, inviting members and assigning roles (Admin, Member), configuring shared Secrets accessible to team Repls, setting up template Repls for consistent project starts, managing billing and subscription for Teams tier, establishing team coding standards and conventions, configuring team-wide environment settings, and auditing team member access and activity. Proper team management enables secure and efficient collaborative development.
