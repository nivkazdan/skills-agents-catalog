### 1. SKILL.md
**Purpose:** Configure Replit workspace with extensions, themes, keybindings, and collaborative settings for optimal productivity.

## Summary

This skill configures the Replit IDE workspace for maximum developer productivity. It covers installing useful extensions from the Replit extension marketplace, customizing themes and color schemes, setting up personalized keybindings, configuring AI assistance preferences, and establishing team sharing settings. The goal is to create a comfortable and efficient development environment tailored to individual or team workflows.
