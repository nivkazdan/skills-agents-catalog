## Summary

**Category:** advanced
**Target Audience:** Creative director or designer
**Trigger Phrases:** `klingai style`, `kling ai artistic`, `stylize klingai`, `klingai anime style`

### What This Skill Does

This skill teaches applying artistic styles to Kling AI video generation. It covers:

- Style categories (anime, oil painting, watercolor, sketch, etc.)
- Cinematic styles (noir, vintage, cyberpunk, fantasy)
- Color grading options (warm, cool, vibrant, muted)
- Style strength control
- Reference image style transfer
- Brand style consistency management

### Technical Success Criteria

- Consistent artistic style across generated videos
- Style strength matching intent
- Brand palette adherence

### Business Success Criteria

- Brand-aligned visual content
- Unique artistic differentiation
- Consistent visual identity
