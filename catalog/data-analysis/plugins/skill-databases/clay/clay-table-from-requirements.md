## File Descriptions

### 1. SKILL.md
**Purpose:** Execute Clay's primary workflow: create enrichment tables from natural language requirements. Translates business requirements into configured Clay tables with appropriate columns and enrichment formulas.
**Workflow:** Core business workflow skill. Use when sales ops or growth teams need to quickly create prospecting lists from requirements.
**Relates to:** Builds on clay-enrichment-patterns; often followed by clay-crm-sync-core for data synchronization.

## Summary

This skill guides users through Clay's signature capability: generating enrichment tables from natural language requirements. It covers analyzing business requirements, selecting appropriate enrichment columns (email, phone, company info, social profiles), configuring enrichment formulas and providers, and setting up the table structure. This is the primary skill for sales ops teams creating prospecting and outreach lists.
