**Target Audience:** Compliance officer

### What This Skill Does

This skill guides compliance reviews for OpenRouter integrations:

- SOC2, HIPAA, GDPR compliance checklists
- Automated compliance scanning tools
- Gap identification and remediation planning
- Evidence collection for audits
- Continuous compliance monitoring

### Technical Success Criteria

- All compliance checklist items verified
- Gaps documented with remediation plans
- Evidence collection automated

### Business Success Criteria

- Pass compliance audits successfully
- Maintain security certifications
- Meet regulatory requirements
