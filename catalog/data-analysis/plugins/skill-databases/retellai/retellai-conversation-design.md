## Summary

**Category:** advanced
**Target Audience:** Conversation designers, UX designers, Product managers
**Trigger Phrases:** `retell conversation design`, `retell dialogue flow`, `retell voice UX`, `retell conversation patterns`

### What This Skill Does

This skill designs effective voice conversation flows for Retell AI agents. It covers conversation architecture with clear user journeys, intent recognition and handling patterns, error recovery and clarification flows, natural dialogue techniques for voice, and testing conversation flows with real users.

### Technical Success Criteria

- Conversation flow documented with decision trees
- Intent handling configured for common user goals
- Error recovery paths defined for confusion scenarios
- Barge-in and interruption handling optimized
- Testing scenarios created for validation

### Business Success Criteria

- Improved user satisfaction with voice experience
- Reduced call abandonment rates
- 80% first-call resolution rate with designed conversation flows
