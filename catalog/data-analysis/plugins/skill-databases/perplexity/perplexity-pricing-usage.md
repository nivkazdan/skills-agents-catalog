## Summary

**Category:** onboarding
**Target Audience:** Developer or project manager
**Trigger Phrases:** `perplexity pricing`, `perplexity cost`, `perplexity usage`, `perplexity budget`

### What This Skill Does

This skill explains Perplexity pricing and optimization:

- Understanding pricing tiers (Free, Pro, Enterprise)
- Per-request pricing and token costs
- Model-specific pricing differences
- Usage tracking and monitoring
- Cost optimization strategies

### Technical Success Criteria

- Clear cost estimates and usage tracking
- Understanding of pricing per model and tier
- Ability to predict monthly costs

### Business Success Criteria

- Controlled spending within budget limits
- Optimal tier selection for needs
- Cost-effective search operations
